Our Method for Preventing Viruses

With a clean and accessible layout, users can easily navigate through topics like "What is a Computer Virus?", "Types of Viruses", "How They Spread", and "How to Stay Safe." This structure promotes self-paced learning and encourages ongoing awareness beyond the classroom.
